# FIRE Portfolio Overlay

**Your brokerage dashboard, upgraded for FIRE investors.**

You check your portfolio every day — but Fidelity, Vanguard, and Schwab only show you what happened, not whether you're on track to retire early. FIRE Portfolio Overlay injects the metrics that actually matter for Financial Independence right onto your brokerage page. No tab-switching. No re-entering data into a spreadsheet. Your FI ratio, projected FIRE date, and milestone progress update live as you watch your balance.

---

## Free Features

### FI Ratio Widget
- Your current **Financial Independence ratio** displayed as a percentage — `portfolio balance ÷ (annual expenses × 25)`
- Color-coded progress bar toward 100% FI
- Updates instantly when your balance refreshes on the page

### Projected FIRE Date
- Calculates months and years to FIRE using your balance, monthly contribution, and expected annual return
- Uses standard compound growth formula — same math as ProjectionLab and cFIREsim
- "Today moved your FIRE date X days closer / further" based on today's gain/loss from your dashboard

### FI Milestone Tracker
- Visual progress toward 25%, 50%, 75%, and 100% FI
- Projected dates for each milestone at your current savings rate
- At-a-glance view of where you are in the FIRE journey

### Setup Wizard (First Run)
- Enter your annual expenses, monthly contribution, expected annual return (default 7%), and target retirement age
- All data stored locally in your browser — never transmitted anywhere
- Takes under 2 minutes to configure; your FIRE metrics appear immediately

## Pro Features — $8/month

### Monte Carlo Success Rate
- 1,000 simulations against Shiller CAPE-adjusted historical return data (1926–2025)
- See your probability of portfolio success under your target withdrawal rate and retirement horizon
- P10/P25/P50/P75/P90 trajectory fan chart — understand best-case and worst-case outcomes

### Sequence of Returns Risk Panel
- Probability of portfolio depletion in the first 5 years of retirement at your current balance and safe withdrawal rate
- The most critical risk factor for early retirees — now visible before you pull the trigger

### Coast FIRE Calculator
- Given your current balance, shows the minimum monthly contribution needed to coast to retirement
- Stop over-saving — or see exactly how far you can throttle back

### Multi-Account Aggregation
- Register multiple brokerages; the extension aggregates your total portfolio across all accounts
- One unified FIRE dashboard regardless of which brokerage tab is open

### Withdrawal Strategy Comparison
- Side-by-side: Fixed SWR vs. Dynamic (Guyton-Klinger) vs. Floor-and-Upside
- Success probability and median annual income for each strategy at your current portfolio
- Make your withdrawal strategy decision with data, not guesswork

## Privacy First

All FIRE calculations happen entirely in your browser. Your balances, expenses, and retirement assumptions never leave your device and are never sent to any server. The privacy statement is visible in the sidebar footer on every page.

- No account required for free tier
- No analytics or tracking
- Settings page includes "Clear all FIRE data" to wipe everything instantly
- Open source architecture — what you see is what runs

2. Visit your Fidelity or Vanguard account summary page

5. Your FI ratio and projected FIRE date appear in the sidebar immediately
