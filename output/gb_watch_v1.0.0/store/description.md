# GB Watch — Mechanical Keyboard Group Buy Monitor

Never miss a group buy again. GB Watch monitors GeekHack, Drop, NovelKeys, and CannonKeys for you — paste a URL, and get notified the instant a GB goes live, closes, or ships.

## Never miss the drop

Mechanical keyboard group buys sell out within hours of going live. GB Watch runs quietly in the background, checking your watched GBs and ICs every 30 minutes and firing a Chrome notification the moment the status changes.

- **GB live** — get notified the instant interest check turns into a full group buy
- **Closing soon** — catch the last few days before a GB ends
- **Shipped** — know when your board is on its way

## Paste a URL, done

No accounts. No sign-ups. Just paste a product or thread URL from a supported store or forum:

- **GeekHack** — GB and IC threads
- **Drop** — keyboard and keycap listings
- **NovelKeys** — in-house and curated GBs
- **CannonKeys** — budget and enthusiast boards

GB Watch detects the status automatically using a content script that reads the page, then stores everything locally in your browser.

## Popup dashboard at a glance

Click the toolbar icon to see all your watched items in one place:

- Item name, source site, and current status badge
- Last-checked timestamp
- Manual "Check all now" refresh button
- Remove items you no longer want to track

## Lightweight and private

- All data stays in your browser — nothing is sent to any server
- No third-party tracking
- Manifest V3
- Minimal permissions: alarms, notifications, storage
