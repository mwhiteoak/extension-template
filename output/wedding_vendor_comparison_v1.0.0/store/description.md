# Wedding Vendor Tracker

**Stop juggling spreadsheets. Track every vendor in your browser.**

Planning a wedding means browsing dozens of photographers, caterers, florists, DJs, and venues across The Knot and WeddingWire — and losing track of them all. Wedding Vendor Tracker fixes that with a single click.

---

## How It Works

1. Browse vendor listings on **The Knot** or **WeddingWire** as normal.
2. Click the **"+ Track Vendor"** button that appears on every listing card.
3. The vendor is instantly saved to your private side panel — name, category, price range, and status.
4. Open the panel any time from the extension icon in your toolbar.

No account required. Your data stays in your browser.

---

## Free Features

- **Track Vendor button** on every listing card — one click to save
- **Persistent side panel** with your full vendor list
- **Status badges**: Contacted / Quoted / Booked / Rejected
- **Notes field** per vendor for free-text reminders
- **CSV export** — download your entire list at any time
- **Filter by category and status**

---

## Pro Features — $7/month

Upgrade to Pro for the full wedding planning toolkit:

- **Side-by-side comparison table** — compare up to 4 vendors per category at a glance
- **Price quote entry** — record the actual quoted price per vendor
- **Budget tracker per category** — set a budget, see how vendor quotes stack up
- **Contact log** — log every touchpoint: date contacted, response received, follow-up date
- **Availability notes** — track whether a vendor is available, waitlisted, or unavailable on your date
- **Email draft shortcut** — one click pre-fills a professional inquiry email to any vendor
- **Cross-device sync** — your data follows you via your Chrome account

---

## Privacy

- No account, no sign-up, no server
- Free tier data stored locally in your browser only (`chrome.storage.local`)
- Pro tier synced privately via your Chrome account (`chrome.storage.sync`)
- No data is ever sent to our servers
