# Homebrew Recipe Sidekick

**Your brewing tools, right on the recipe page.**

Home brewers on homebrewtalk.com and brewersfriend.com switch tabs constantly — looking up hop substitutes, running IBU calculations, checking grain color, converting units. Homebrew Recipe Sidekick injects a collapsible sidebar directly onto the page, putting every tool you need in context while you're reading the recipe. No tab-switching. No re-entering data you can already see on screen.

---

## Free Features

### Hop Substitution Lookup
- Type any hop name, get substitutes with alpha acid adjustment notes — instantly
- 500+ hops in a local database, no internet connection required
- Alpha acid % matched so your IBU stays on target when substituting

### IBU Calculator (Tinseth Formula)
- Input hop weight, alpha acid %, boil time, and batch size
- Auto-populates from recipe fields detected on homebrewtalk.com and brewersfriend.com when available
- Displays total IBU and per-addition breakdown

### ABV Calculator
- OG and FG inputs → ABV output
- Supports both standard formula and alternate (more accurate for high-gravity beers)
- Detects OG/FG from structured recipe pages automatically

### SRM Color Estimator
- Input grain weights and Lovibond ratings → estimated SRM (Standard Reference Method)
- Visual color swatch matches the calculated value
- Uses the Morey equation — the industry standard for homebrewing

### Batch Size & Unit Converter
- Set your unit preference once (Imperial or Metric) — persists across sessions
- Instant conversion: gallons ↔ liters, oz ↔ grams, °F ↔ °C, Plato ↔ specific gravity
- Converts recipe quantities on the fly when your batch size differs from the recipe

## Pro Features — $5/month

### Water Chemistry Calculator
- Target water profiles (Pilsen, Burton, Dublin, or custom) → mineral additions to hit your target
- Supports gypsum, calcium chloride, baking soda, and Epsom salt
- Reads your source water profile; shows addition quantities in grams per gallon/liter
- All calculations local — your water data never leaves your browser

### Yeast Attenuation Estimator
- OG + yeast strain attenuation range → expected FG range and ABV spread
- Auto-reads OG from the page when detected; great for high-gravity and lager planning
- Useful for understanding how much fermentability matters for your target ABV

### Recipe Saving (up to 20 recipes)
- Save and name recipe snapshots (ingredients + calculated values) from any session
- Stored locally in your browser — your recipes are private and never transmitted
- One-click reload: saved recipes repopulate all calculator fields instantly

### BrewersFriend Recipe Import
- One-click parse of structured recipe data on brewersfriend.com recipe pages
- Auto-fills all calculator inputs — OG, hops, grain bill, batch size — in one action
- Eliminates re-entry when working from a BrewersFriend recipe

## Privacy First

All calculations happen locally in your browser. No recipe data, gravity readings, or personal information ever leaves your device. No account required for the free tier. No analytics. Settings page includes "Clear All Saved Data" to wipe everything instantly.

## Setup

1. Install the extension
2. Visit any recipe thread on homebrewtalk.com or a recipe page on brewersfriend.com
3. The sidebar appears at the right edge of the page — click to expand
4. Hop lookup and calculators are ready immediately — no setup required
