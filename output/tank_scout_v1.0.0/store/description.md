# Tank Scout — Fish Compatibility Checker

Tank Scout automatically detects fish species on aquarium shopping pages and shows you everything you need to know before you buy.

---

## What it does

- Detects fish species names on LiveAquaria, Aquabid, and eBay listings
- Shows minimum tank size, pH range, and temperature requirements
- Rates care difficulty from Easy to Expert
- Checks compatibility against your saved tank — warns about conflicts before you buy

---

## Your Tank Profile

Save your current fish and tank setup in one click. Tank Scout remembers your fish, tank size, and water parameters so every listing gets a personalized compatibility check.

**Compatibility checks include:**
- Aggression mismatch warnings
- Tank size requirements
- pH and temperature parameter conflicts
- Care difficulty rating

---

## Inline Compatibility Panel

On every fish listing, Tank Scout injects a compact panel showing:

- Species common name and scientific name
- Minimum tank size (gallons)
- Water parameters: pH range and temperature range
- Care difficulty badge: Easy / Moderate / Difficult / Expert
- Live compatibility check: ✅ Compatible / ⚠️ Caution / ❌ Conflict — with specific conflict reasons

---

## Powered by trusted data

52 species bundled locally, sourced from Seriously Fish and FishBase — the same references used by professional aquarists worldwide. Background service worker fetches and caches extended species data with a 7-day TTL for offline resilience.

## Privacy

No account required. No data leaves your browser. Tank profile stored locally via `chrome.storage.local`.
